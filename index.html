Mother Teresa

1910 – 1997

▶

"Not all of us can do great things. But we can do small things with great love."

What I Adore in Her

Her boundless compassion, tireless service to the poor, and ability to see dignity in every soul.

I admire her calm spirit, humility, and dedication to love without judgment.

Her actions were louder than words — she lived the message of peace and care.

Biography

Born in Macedonia, Mother Teresa became a symbol of humanitarian work through her mission in India.

She founded the Missionaries of Charity, dedicating her life to the sick, the orphaned, and the dying.

She was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979 and was later canonized as Saint Teresa of Calcutta.
